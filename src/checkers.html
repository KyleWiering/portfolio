📝 How to Play

Goal: Capture all of your opponent's pieces or block them so they cannot move.

Basic Rules:

  • Players take turns moving one piece at a time.
  • Pieces move diagonally forward to an adjacent empty square.
  • Capture opponent pieces by jumping over them diagonally to an empty square.
  • When a piece reaches the far row, it becomes a King and can move backward as well.

🖱️ Controls

Desktop: Click on a piece to select it, then click on a highlighted square to move.

Mobile: Tap a piece to select, then tap a highlighted square to move.

⚙️ Settings

Adjust game options such as sound, board style, or restart the game from the menu.
